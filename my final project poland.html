About Poland

Poland officially the republic of poland ; is a country located in central Europe.Poland's territory extends from the Baltic Sea in the north to the Sudetes and Carpathian Mountains in the south. The country is bordered by Lithuania and Russia (Kaliningrad Oblast) to the northeast, Belarus and Ukraine to the east, Slovakia and the Czech Republic to the south, and Germany to the westIt is divided into 16 administrative provinces ,covering an area of 312,696 Km2.Poland has a population of nearly 38.5 million,and warsaw is the nation's capital and largest metropolis.
